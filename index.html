Meet Your Doctor

Dr. Antonio Perez-Duran

Dr. Perez-Duran is the clinic director of RxWellness Tysons. He is a Full-Body Certified Active Release Techniques provider, certified by the Titleist Performance Institute, and trained in Dry Needling, RockTape Fascial Movement Taping, and Selective Functional Movement Assessment.

Originally from Puerto Rico, Dr. Perez-Duran earned his Bachelor's in Biology from Boston College, became an EMT at George Washington University (licensed by NREMT), attended medical school at Universidad Central del Caribe, and received his Doctor of Chiropractic degree from New York Chiropractic College.
